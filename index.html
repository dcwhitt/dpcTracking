DPC Distance

Measure distal palmar crease distance from a smartphone photo using a U.S. penny for scale.

✋

This tool estimates the distance from the fingertip to the distal palmar crease on a hand photo. Upload a photo, calibrate the image using a U.S. penny, then click the fingertip and distal palmar crease for each finger you want to measure.

Photo tip: Place a U.S. penny flat in the same plane as the hand. Keep the camera straight-on, avoid angled shots, and make sure the fingertip, distal palmar crease, and penny are all visible.

How to Use

  1. Upload a photo with a U.S. penny visible.

  3. Click the opposite edge of the penny.
  4. Select the finger you want to measure.
  5. Click the fingertip.
  6. Click the distal palmar crease point.
  7. Repeat for other fingers.
